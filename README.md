# 🏨 Hotel Booking Platform – Strategic Business Review

> A comprehensive Business Analyst case study focused on improving revenue, reducing cancellations, and increasing profitability using data-driven decision-making.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end strategic business review of a hotel booking platform using **30,000 booking transactions** collected over a 13-month period (April 2024 – April 2025).

The objective was to analyze booking behavior, identify revenue leakage, evaluate operational performance, and recommend strategic initiatives to improve business growth and profitability.

The project follows a real-world consulting approach by combining business analysis, KPI reporting, root cause analysis, financial impact estimation, and executive-level recommendations.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The platform was experiencing:

- High booking cancellation rates
- Revenue leakage
- Booking failures
- Underutilized premium inventory
- Inefficient sales channels
- Margin optimization challenges

The goal was to identify the root causes and recommend practical, data-backed business strategies.
